Why is SunMone the name for this project?

My name's Phuong Tien. I'm studying Information Technology at HUFI. SunMone project is my brainchild cherished all this time. The reason for taking that name because "Sun" is the sun, "Mone" is the last name, whose surname Mone means active, cheerful person. Here, the SunMone brand that I want to aim for is to help everyone who wears SunMone's clothes feel happy and ahine like the sun.
